My Activities!

Running, Charities, Teaching

I find running extraordinary as it is the medicine my mind craves after a long day. A hard afterschool workout can drain me, but a nice run, with a regulated pace, breath, and heartbeat, always makes me feel whole. During these runs, I often think of Marcus Aurelius's words "It is in your own power to maintain the beauty of your soul or to be a decent human being.". In the quiet rhythm,I feel a connection to this truth. The simplicity of running helps me reflect, clear my mind. Each stride reminds me of how I navigate my inner world, nurturing resilience, compassion, and the strength to be a better version of myself. *The image was taken when I attended the Techcombank HCMC midnight run.

My experience in "Darling, Darling, Santa is Coming 2023" was both challenging and heartwarming. The preparation involved coordinating donations, planning activities, and ensuring everything would create a joyful, welcoming atmosphere for the community. The experience deepened my appreciation for giving back, especially during the holiday season when bringing happiness to others is the greatest gift. *Image was taken when I was preparing Christmas tree made of velvet wire.

Engaging with young learners and witnessing their curiosity about technology was inspiring. I realized how powerful even basic digital skills can be in today’s world, and it felt meaningful to share knowledge that could empower them in the future. As we went through each lesson, I saw their confidence grow, which was incredibly rewarding. *image taken in that ICT room.

Having participated and organized the Howartz Summer Camp in An Giang Province, VietNam was truly an incredibly rewarding experience. Not only I got to work with curious students from different background, I also learnt and proved myself in mentoring, organising classes, and most importantly explaining myself thoroughly to achild. It was from that trip I have realized the necessity of being able to read and write in life and I also promised myself to spend time reading everyday. *That image was taken from my Summer Camp teaching basic English writing and speaking skills to ethnic minority-children.
